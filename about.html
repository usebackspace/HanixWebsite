Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a global B2B leader

Founded in 2012, Hanix began as a small workshop with a vision to redefine bag manufacturing for businesses worldwide. Over the years, our relentless commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has transformed us into a trusted partner for global brands, corporations, and distributors. Today, Hanix stands at the forefront of the industry, delivering custom solutions and large-scale production with a personal touch.
